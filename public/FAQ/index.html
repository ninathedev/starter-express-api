I just found a bug! How do I report?

You may do a bugreport by:
1. Are you on a mobile? If yes, sorry, you can't do the steps here. Go to the other question.
2. On the website that receives the error, (if you are on a 500 or 404 page, go back to previous page), type F12 (the key, not the code).
3. Go to console
4. Screenshot the console (you may click on the arrows to show some stuff clearly)
5. Send them to Machica or Tafalla

But I am on mobile? How to I report then?

Tell Machica on how to replicate this issue (I prefer step-by-step instructions).
